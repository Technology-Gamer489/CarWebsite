About Me

Hi! I'm Callum, the designer and developer of this fan-made website! I'm a massive fan of cars and in particular, the Gran Turismo series (this is certainly a way to show it)! I remember first playing Gran Turismo 3 on the PS2, before having Gran Turismo 4 bought for me. Let me tell you, I played that game a LOT when I was a kid. Unfortunately, I didn't get a PS3, so I didn't play GT5 or 6, but I have played Gran Turismo Sport and GT7!

Here is some general stuff about me that's related to this website:

  • I have a degree in Computer Science
  • Apart from learning web development in learning environments, I'm self taught
  • I've been able to name the brands of cars since I was around 2 years old - my mom has always told me that when we get into a topic about cars
  • I'm very good at those quizzes that show badges from a car manufacturer and you have to guess which it is (this is a weird flex, I know)
  • This website is not only intended to show cars and details about them, it's also a way to provide access for people (not just car lovers) who might not be able to access some websites because they don't have appropriate accessibility features in place
